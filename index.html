About the founder

Louisa Nixon is a visionary entrepreneur and dedicated advocate for turning everyday ideas into impactful realities. With a deep passion for people and their potential, she understands that not everyone gets the chance to pursue their dreams, and she is committed to helping them bring their ideas to the limelight.


In 2023, Louisa founded 360synergy Coaching Firm with the mission to develop and implement strategies that ensure ideas reach their full potential. Under her leadership, the firm focuses on providing comprehensive support in areas such as social media management, grant writing, project supervision, and business development.


With a background in law and business management, Louisa brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to her role. Her approach is holistic, combining innovative thinking with practical execution to deliver exceptional results. To date, she has successfully collaborated with over 10 brands, helping them transform their visions into reality.


Beyond her professional achievements, Louisa is also an advocate for continuous learning and personal growth. She regularly engages in workshops and seminars to stay ahead of industry trends and bring the latest insights to her clients.


Her dedication to excellence and unwavering support has made her a trusted partner for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to scale new heights
